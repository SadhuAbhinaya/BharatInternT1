PROJECTS

SMART BOREWELL

A smart bore well provided with a lid comprises an ultrasonic sensor, an arduino board, a servo motor, bary module and custom controlled software application. The ultrasonic sensor measures the distance using basic sound waves. The arduino board provided to receive signals from sensor and operate the servo motor in closing the lid. The servo motors are used for precise control of angular position, velocity and acceleration The ultrasonic sensor alerts the arduino by detecting the approaching to operate the servo motor in closing of lid

TO DO LIST

A to do list is a list of tasks you set out to do ordered according to the level of importance. It is an important part of planning big events, projects, and classwork. It helps you delegate tasks, space your time, and keep your priorities in order. It also gives you a clear picture of what you need to get done and a peace of mind that you have a plan in place. A to do list can be made the night before or first thing in the morning. It can be used by different people from different spheres. However, it may lack clarity and prioritization and sometimes lead to procrastination if not used properly
